Scarcity Score

Our scarcity score is a represenation of a coin's circulating supply relative to other currencies on the market. The SS range spands from 1 to 5, with one being the least scarce and 5 being the most scarce. While pretty straight forward, our scaricty score provides investors with a simple solution to understanding supply metrics.
